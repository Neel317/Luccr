About us

Our Vision

Grounded in the “Seven I’s” Framework, LUCCR uses state of the art scholarly insights to help organizations communicate risk information more effectively to promote consumer, community, societal, and environmental well-being.

Our Mission

LUCCR:
(i) Provides organizations around the globe with interactive access to a wide range of rigorous academic research on understanding and communicating risk,
(ii) Offers normative solutions from these literatures, and
(iii) converts scholarly research into a customized set of structured output for practitioners.
Developed by a team of PhD’s, with the input of executives, small business owners, and policy experts, LUCCR assists professionals who aim to communicate risk-related information across a variety of organizations, industries, and contexts with the goal of promoting individual and societal well-being. With that, LUCCR helps strengthen the connections between academic research and managerial practice.
